Our Events

The Model Club of BIT Sindri has been fostering technical excellence and management skills since its inception in 1977. With its motto, "Be Technical by Technique," the club conducts a diverse range of events, workshops, guest lectures, and mentorship programs to enhance both technical and personal development.

One of its biggest contributions is SANDHAAN, the official techno-management fest, which comprises 23 unique segments, including Techie of the Year, Senior & Junior Scientica, The CEO, Robo Seguidor, Da Vinci Code, and Bike Assembling. These events test participants' innovation, analytical thinking, and problem-solving abilities across various domains.

Beyond Sandhaan, the club has organized several standalone competitions and programs such as:

  • Case-O-Heist - A strategic business and problem-solving event.
  • E-Hackathon - A competitive coding and software development challenge.
  • Model Mentorship Program - A knowledge-sharing initiative to guide budding engineers.
  • De Vision - A visionary event promoting futuristic ideas and creativity.
  • Virtual Science and Technology Fair - A digital platform for showcasing scientific innovations.
  • Listiquiz - A quiz competition testing technical and general knowledge.
  • What If - A thought-provoking challenge exploring hypothetical technological scenarios.
  • The CEO 3.0 - A leadership-based event simulating business decision-making.
